With every highlights and hair color service, we offer a free eyebrow waxing experience. Eyebrow waxing is a beauty treatment that involves using a specially formulated wax to remove unwanted hair from the eyebrows and shape them according to the client's desired look. It is a quick and precise method for achieving well-defined and symmetrical eyebrows. It can enhance the overall look of the face and complement a new hair color or highlights.
